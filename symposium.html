Multiphase Flows Symposium


The 5th International Conference on Fluid Flow, Heat and Mass Transfer (FFHMT'18) invites you to join the Multiphase Flows Symposium.

Multiphase flows found in a wide spectrum of engineering applications such as power generation, nuclear, oil production, water treatment, water desalination, refrigeration and air conditioning, carbon capture and sequestration as well as in processing equipment such as food, chemical processing, material handling and others. The Multiphase Flows Symposium, organized under the 5th International Conference of Fluid Flow, Heat and Mass Transfer (FFHMT'18), will be a forum of discussion of the industrial applications of multiphase flow technology for researchers and engineers working in different scientific and industrial sectors.

The symposium solicits contributions of abstracts, papers or posters that address themes and topics in the area of multiphase flow including but not limited to:

  • Multiphase flow simulation and experimentation
  • Boiling and Condensation
  • Bubble and drop dynamics
  • Cavitation
  • Interface phenomena
  • Flow in porous media
  • Fluidized beds
  • Atomization

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us at info@ffhmt.com.

Workshop


The 5th International Conference on Fluid Flow, Heat and Mass Transfer (FFHMT'18) invites you to propose a workshop.

The proposed workshop, if accepted, will be co-located with and under the umbrella of the conference.

Proposals by prospective chairs should be submitted to info@ffhmt.com and will be evaluated by the Organizing and Scientific Committees. Upon approval, the Organizing Committee will disseminate the news through the conference website and call for papers. To obtain a high attendance, we kindly ask that the prospective chairs also publicize the event and personally contact researchers who they think will be interested to contribute. The minimum number of registered presenters for the event to take place is 7. Should attendance reach this minimum requirement, registration fees for the Chair will be waived.

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us at info@ffhmt.com.
