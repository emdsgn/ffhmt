Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Rashal Abed
    for the paper entitled: " Two-phase Flow Behaviour in Airlift Pumps"
  • Georg Liesche
    for the paper entitled:"Conduction-Convection-Radiation Heat Transfer in High Temperature Catalytic Reactors"
  • Shaker Bukhari
    for the paper entitled: "Multiphase Airlift Pumps for Water circulation in Fish Farms"
  • Dr. Karsten Schwalbe
    for the paper entitled: " Optimal Control of a Stirling Engine"
