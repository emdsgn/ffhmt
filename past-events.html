Past Events

The previous event in this conference series, FFHMT, was held on August 7 - 9, 2017, in Toronto, Canada. Our year-long planning and organization of several committees resulted in the conference becoming a major success. Please see below some of the highlights of the event.

Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Behnam Dastvareh
    for the paper entitled: " Instabilities of Nanoflow Displacements in Homogeneous Porous Media"
  • Dr. Behnam Mostajeran Goortani
    for the paper entitled:"Fabrication of Low Cost Composite Spargers and Their Performance in Polar and Non-Polar Liquids"
  • Huan Jang Keh
    for the paper entitled: "Thermophoresis of an Aerosol Particle in a Microtube"
  • Dr. Jules Thibault
    for the paper entitled: " Modelling of Mixed Matrix Membranes Using a Resistance-Based Model"
  • Mohammad Zargartalebi
    for the paper entitled: "Nanoflow Miscible Viscous Fingering in Real Porous Media: A Mesoscopic Approach"
